API Documentation

class dog_breeds.Breed

This class defines a dog breed

describe_breed()

Shows the breed name and group

Usage:

>>> import dog_breeds
>>> dog = dog_breeds.Labrador() 
>>> dog.describe_breed()  
This dog is a Labrador Retriever.
It is from Newfoundland.
>>>
class dog_breeds.Golden

The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed of retriever dog of medium size. Along with Labrador, Toller and WSS is one of the VIII group: Retrievers - Flushing Dogs - Water Dogs.

class dog_breeds.Labrador

The Labrador Retriever or simply Labrador is a British breed of retriever gun dog. Along with Golden, Toller and WSS is one of the VIII group: Retrievers - Flushing Dogs - Water Dogs

class dog_breeds.Toller

The Nova Scotia Duck Tolling Retriever is a medium-sized gundog bred primarily for hunting. Along with Golden, Labrador and WSS is one of the VIII group: Retrievers - Flushing Dogs - Water Dogs.

class dog_breeds.WSS

The Welsh Springer Spaniel is a breed of dog and a member of the spaniel family. Along with Golden, Toller and Labrador is one of the VIII group: Retrievers - Flushing Dogs - Water Dogs.
